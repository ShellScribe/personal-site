I like infrastructure that explains itself.

That usually means fewer mystery paths, fewer one-off fixes, cleaner ownership, better automation, and enough context for another engineer to understand why something was built the way it was.

My work sits across cloud platforms, networks, security controls, deployment systems, and the operational habits that keep enterprise environments moving.

## Focus Areas

- Practical automation.
- Secure operations.
- Reliable delivery paths.
- Cross-layer troubleshooting.
