# Welcome

This is the starter version of my personal site.

## Next step: template selection

After the core content is live, I will evaluate alternate Jekyll templates based on:

1. **Readability** for long-form writing.
2. **Performance** on mobile.
3. **Customization cost** for branding and navigation.
4. **Maintenance quality** (recent updates, issue responsiveness).

I will compare 2-3 themes against these criteria and migrate once the baseline site structure is stable.
